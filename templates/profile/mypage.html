Welcome to TaskMaster!

Your all-in-one solution for managing projects and tasks. TaskMaster is designed to help you stay organized and collaborate efficiently with your team. Here's what you can do:


  • Edit Personal Information: Update your profile to keep your account information up to date.

  • Create Projects: Organize your work by creating projects. Each project has its own dedicated group chat for seamless team communication.

  • Create and Manage Tasks: Break down your work into tasks within each project. Assign tasks, set priorities, and track progress.

  • Search and Filter Tasks: Easily find tasks by filtering based on priority and task members.

  • AI Integration: The AI assesses the detail level of task content upon creation and generates a comprehensive explanation of the task's contents

  • Account Settings: Change your account details anytime
